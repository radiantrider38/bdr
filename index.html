THE MOTIVE

Revenge for Border Clashes

India's alleged motive traces to escalating BDR-BSF tensions in the late 90s. The Pilkhana massacre is cited by the 2025 Commission as a "decapitation strike" to avenge Indian losses (Boraibari & Padua) and install a pliable regime in Dhaka.

"A repeat of Plassey... they killed the best of us to rule the rest of us."

- Maj. Gen. (retd) ALM Fazlur Rahman

Boraibari Clash (1999)

TRIGGER POINT

BDR killed 16 Indian BSF jawans in a disputed enclave. RAW reportedly initiated long-term destabilization plans immediately after this humiliation.

Padua-Roumari (2001)

ESCALATION

BDR successfully defended territory, killing 16 BSF personnel. This successful defense by the BDR earned the permanent ire of RAW, fueling the vendetta to destroy the force.

THE CHRONOLOGY OF BETRAYAL

WARNING: GRAPHIC DETAILS INSIDE

FEB 24 - 10:00 AM

The Setup

PM Hasina visits Pilkhana. Intelligence ignores reports of missing SMGs. Agitators positioned.

FEB 25 - 09:02 AM

The First Shot

Soldiers storm the Darbar Hall. DG Shakil Ahmed attempts to calm them but is insulted.

FEB 25 - 09:26 AM

The Phone Call

DG Shakil calls PM Hasina. She allegedly does not pick up initially. When connected, no order for rescue is given.

FEB 25 - 10:30 AM (CRITICAL)

THE STAND DOWN ORDER

Brigade 46 and RAB were at the gates. Hasina orders Army Chief Moeen U Ahmed to halt operations for "political negotiations."

This 4-hour delay allowed the systematic execution of 57 officers in the Darbar Hall.

FEB 25 - 12:30 PM

The White Flag Deception

Taposh and Nanak arrive with white flags. Instead of rescuing officers, they negotiate amnesty for the killers.

FEB 25 - 03:00 PM

The Residence Meeting

Hasina meets 14 mutineer leaders at her Jamuna residence. Amnesty granted while officers are being buried in mass graves.

FEB 26 - MORNING

Discovery

Tanks finally enter. Mass graves found. Bodies mutilated. 57 Officers confirmed dead.
